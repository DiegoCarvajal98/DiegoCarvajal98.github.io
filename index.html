About

Diego A. Carvajal S., BSc.

I am a Mechatronics Engineer with hands-on experience designing and integrating robotic systems, specializing in both mobile wheeled robots and robotic arms, leveraging strong expertise in ROS with Python and C++. My passion lies in solving complex challenges through programming, systems design, and integration, creating robust solutions that enhance efficiency and effectiveness.

I have a proven track record of working in a fast-paced environment, integrating hardware and software, and developing software for robotic platforms. I have solid experience with industrial components, such as PLCs and HMIs, which ensures a comprehensive approach to system development.

I am a collaborative team player with a passion for continuous learning and problem-solving. I am eager to apply my skills to innovative and challenging projects in robotics and automation.
